who are we?

get to know us

We are a bunch of teens who believe that no matter how much we impose the idea of social media being a bad influence on all of us, it is essential to understand the importance of it at the same time during such critical times. So, we aim to reach out to all the adults and children out there to aid us in our objective of highlighting both how advantageous and disadvantageous social media is to our daily lives.
